About the company

Canadian Solar Solutions Inc. is a global company based in Guelph, Ontario, Canada which engages in manufacturing solar photovoltaic (PV) modules and provides solar energy solutions worldwide. It offers solar cells, solar modules, solar power systems, off-grid home application system and other specialized solar products.

The office located in Guelph has a manufacturing facility where solar cells, solar PV modules, etc. are manufactured. I had the opportunity to have a tour around the facility and witness the production of solar panels.

The department where I was assigned during my co-op (Operations and Maintenance) consists of people from different backgrounds: control room operators, system performance analysts, developers, engineers, etc. working closely together to provide monitoring, diagnostics and troubleshooting of solar plants by using one of their software, namely CSEye.
