About the company

Canadian Solar Solutions Inc. is a global company based in Guelph, Ontario, Canada which engages in manufacturing solar photovoltaic (PV) modules and provides solar energy solutions worldwide. It offers solar cells, solar modules, solar power systems, off-grid home application system and other specialized solar products.

The office located in Guelph has a manufacturing facility where solar cells, solar PV modules, etc. are manufactured. I had the opportunity to have a tour around the facility and witness the production of solar panels.

The department where I was assigned during my co-op (Operations and Maintenance) consists of people from different backgrounds: control room operators, system performance analysts, developers, engineers, etc. working closely together to provide monitoring, diagnostics and troubleshooting of solar plants by using one of their software, namely CSEye.

Goals

For my first work term, I set 4 goals whose main purposes were to help focusing what I would like to achieve at the end of my work term and also enable me to track my progress.

  • Goal 1
  • Goal 2
  • Goal 3
  • Goal 4

Goal 1:

Use previous experience in C and Java to learn new language, Python and how web frameworks work, Django, to improve backend development skills.

Reflection:

Improved backend development skills and successfully made use of Django to implement backend of project.

Gained working experience with MySQL on how to write queries and on how the data is being saved in the database and retrieved using Django Query set.

Goal 2:

Acquire front-end development skills and learn how to use front- web development technologies, such as Angular together with HTML ,CSS, JavaScript and Bootstrap.

Reflection:

Successfully acquired front-end development skills and became familiar with front-end web technologies such as HTML,CSS, bootstrap, JavaScript/JQuery/Ajax.

Goal 3:

Gain experience working in a team of programmers and further develop team work skills which will benefit my future career.

Reflection:

Gained working experience by regularly discussing and sharing ideas with mentors and getting help and feedback. Furthermore, code review sessions helped improving both my coding skills and communication skills.

Goal 4:

Gain work experience in computer science field and understand on how projects are conducted, how programmers choose the tools to use and take steps to bring project to completion; and how each individual effort contributes to the success of the project.

Reflection:

Gained a better understanding on the steps taken to design and implement a project through meetings where developers show their progress, demonstrate tools that they have used to implement certain features and ask for feedbacks and suggestions.
